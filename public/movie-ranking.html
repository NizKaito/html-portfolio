The Best Movie According to Nizam

My top 4 Movie of all time

No 1. The Dark Knight

When a menace known as the Joker wreaks havoc and chaos on the people of
Gotham, Batman, James Gordon and Harvey Dent
must work together to put an end to the madness.

No 2. The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King

Gandalf and Aragorn lead the World of Men against Sauron's army to draw his gaze from
Frodo and Sam as they approach Mount Doom with the One Ring.

No 3. Inception

A thief who steals corporate secrets through the use of dream-sharing
technology is given the inverse task of planting an idea into the mind of a C.E.O.,
but his tragic past may doom the project and his team to disaster.

No 4. The Pianist

During WWII, acclaimed Polish musician Wladyslaw faces various struggles
as he loses contact with his family. As the situation worsens,
he hides in the ruins of Warsaw in order to survive.
